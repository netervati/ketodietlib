Here are neat tricks to optimize your search results:

  • To search on the name column only, add : at the beginning of the search bar.
  • To search on the name column and match the exact keyword only, add :: at the beginning of the search bar.
  • To search on the group column only, add * at the beginning of the search bar.
  • To search on the group column and match the exact keyword only, add ** at the beginning of the search bar.
Note: These are case-sensitive.
